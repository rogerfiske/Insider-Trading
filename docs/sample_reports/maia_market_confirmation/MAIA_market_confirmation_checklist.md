## 10. Data Quality Limitations

**Price/volume data:**
- If live market data is not integrated, all price and volume values are **manual entry** and subject to human error.
- Historical price/volume data may be unavailable or require external source integration.
- Bid-ask spread and intraday volatility are not captured unless manually noted.

**SEC filing timing:**
- Form 4 filings due within 2 business days of transaction; price reaction may precede public filing.
- Form 144 filings indicate **intent to sell**, not completed sale; actual sale timing is not disclosed.
- 13F-HR filings have 45-day lag from quarter-end; holdings data is stale.
- 10-Q/10-K filings may be delayed or amended; always verify latest accession number.

**13F institutional visibility:**
- Parser success rate is 60% (CP23F-Fix); 2 of 5 large managers could not be parsed.
- No-match conclusion is scoped to successfully parsed filings only.
- 13F only reports long positions >$200k or >10k shares threshold.
- Derivatives, shorts, and synthetic positions not fully visible.

**Clinical timing:**
- THIO-104 Phase 3 data timing is **not disclosed**; this is the **critical unknown** in the synthesis.
- Clinical updates depend on MAIA voluntary disclosure; no automatic source exists.

**Insider activity:**
- Open-market purchases are visible via Form 4.
- Private transactions, option exercises, and restricted stock grants are also in Form 4 but require manual filtering.
- Rule 10b5-1 trading plans are not always disclosed; cannot distinguish planned vs. discretionary trades.
